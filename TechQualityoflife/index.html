My quality of life has been improved with technology.

About

Technology can lead to "the good life."

This website is about how technology has improved my own quality of life. I will show you some examples.

Examples of Technology

Health

I want to live a healthy life. I use a fitbit to track my steps to be healthy. I can also track calories lost/consumed, hours of sleep, heart rate, as well as connect with my friends.

Career

My job is to teach computer science. Technology and computer science interconnect. This career has given me a wonderful life so far.

Travel

I love to travel, I book all of my trips via apps or online. One of the most innovative tech is the Airbnb app, I can stay in a great place for an affordable price.

Home

Technology has given the ability for me to purchase a house. It has also allowed me to fix things as well as live in a comfortable and safe environment.
